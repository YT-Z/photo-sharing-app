Story number and name: 4 - Activity feed
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action: 
Click on a link on the toolbar labelled "Activities" and view the 5 most recent activities 
that have happened on the web site in reverse chronological order (most recent at the top).
Click on a refresh icon to trigger an update.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Story number and name: 6 - Photo like votes
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action: 
Click on a thumbsup icon to like a photo. The icon will change into thumbsdown if the user 
has liked the photo. Click on the thumbsdown icon to unlike the photo. Next to the icon, 
it displays the number of likes for the photo. A user's photo page is sorted by the number
of likes in decending order. Photos of the same number of likes whill be sorted by the 
timestamp in reverse chronological order.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Story number and name: 7 - Favorite lists of photos for users
Story points: 6
Brief description to see the story in action:
Click on a heart icon to favorite a photo. The icon will be disabled when a photo
is favorited and there will be a word "favorited" next to the icon to incate that this photo 
has been favorited by the user.
Click on a link on the toolbar labelled "Favorites" and view a list of photos the user has 
favorited. Each photo is a small thumbnail. Clicking on the thumbnail cause a modal window 
to open up displaying a larger version of the image. 
Click on the far right X to unfavorite a photo.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Story number and name: 8 - Sidebar list marks users with new activity
Story points: 3
Brief description to see the story in action:
The user list on the nav sidebar will show the last activity done by each user, including
phosted a photo, commented a photo, registered, logged in, logged out, like/unlike a photo,
favorited/unfavorited a photo. The display will update automatically.
